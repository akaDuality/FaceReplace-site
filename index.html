Manifesto
Your face is the most personal data you own. So we wrote an app that never sends it anywhere.
From the README — released day one
  • 01
    Local model, local memory. Inference runs on the Neural Engine of the device you're holding. No round-trip, no API key.
  • 02
    Air-gapped by default. Turn off Wi-Fi and the app keeps working. The only network call is a Mac handoff, on your LAN.
  • 03
    iCloud is yours. Sync uses your private iCloud container. We never see the contents — Apple holds the keys, not us.
  • 04
    No analytics on faces. We don't log inputs, outputs, or detection results. The app could run inside a Faraday cage.
Continuity

Phone in your hand,
render on your Mac.

Long clip and a hot iPhone? Tap "Run on Mac." The phone discovers your Mac on the local network, streams the source, and the desktop's Apple silicon takes the heat. Your Mac keeps the file ready in its own gallery — and pushes the result back via iCloud.
